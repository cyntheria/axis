AXIS Resampler

A blazingly-fast, high-fidelity UTAU resampler written in Rust.

AXIS is meant to be highly advanced yet lightweight with high amount of portability and speed, It uses the WORLD vocoder (via rsworld), AXIS can run on OpenUtau and UTSU (possibly UTAU aswell, untested.)

AXIS is licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License v3.0. This ensures the engine remains free and open while allowing for seamless integration as a library or binary for both OpenUtau and UTSU.
